Milk

Amul Milk, sourced from select dairy farms, is known for its purity and freshness. It undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure safety and nutrition. Available in different variants like full cream, toned, and double-toned, Amul Milk caters to diverse dietary needs.

Butter

Amul butter, made from pasteurized cream and salt, is known for its rich, creamy taste and smooth texture. It is available in various sizes, from small individual packs to larger family packs. The brand is iconic in India, recognized for its "Utterly Butterly Delicious" tagline and the Amul Girl mascot. Widely used for spreading, cooking, and baking, Amul butter is a staple in Indian households..

Dahi

Amul Dahi, a product of the Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation (GCMMF), is a popular and trusted brand of yogurt in India. It is made from pasteurized, homogenized toned milk, offering a creamy texture and a mildly tangy flavor. Available in various sizes, Amul Dahi is commonly used in Indian households for direct consumption, in recipes, and for preparing traditional dishes like raita and lassi. It is known for its high quality and consistent taste.

Icecream

Amul Ice Cream, produced by the Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation (GCMMF), is renowned for its rich, creamy texture and wide range of flavors. Made from fresh milk and natural ingredients, it is a popular choice in India for its quality and taste. Amul offers various varieties, including classic flavors, fruit-based options, and innovative combinations.

Ghee

Amul Ghee, produced by the Gujarat Cooperative Milk Marketing Federation (GCMMF), is made from high-quality butter derived from pure cow's milk. It is known for its rich aroma, granular texture, and authentic taste, making it a staple in Indian cooking and rituals. Available in various sizes, Amul Ghee is used for frying, as a condiment, and in traditional sweets.

Chaas

Amul Chaas, also known as Amul Buttermilk, is a refreshing, lightly spiced beverage made from curd. It's known for its cooling properties and digestive benefits, making it a popular choice in India, especially during hot weather. Amul Chaas is available in convenient, ready-to-drink packaging, perfect for on-the-go consumption.

Cream

Amul Fresh Cream is made from high-quality milk, offering a rich and creamy texture suitable for various culinary uses. It is commonly used in both sweet and savory dishes, including desserts, soups, and sauces. Available in convenient tetra packs, Amul Fresh Cream ensures long shelf life and easy storage.

Chocolate

Amul Chocolate is made from high-quality cocoa and milk, offering a rich and smooth taste. It comes in various flavors, including dark, milk, and fruit & nut, catering to different preferences. Known for its premium quality, Amul Chocolate is a popular choice for both gifting and personal indulgence.

About Amul

Amul is an Indian dairy cooperative society, based at Anand in the state of Gujarat. Formed in 1946, it is a brand managed by a cooperative body, the Gujarat Co-operative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd. (GCMMF), which today is jointly owned by 3.6 million milk producers in Gujarat.
