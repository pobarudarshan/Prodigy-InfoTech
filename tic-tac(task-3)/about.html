About Tic-Tac Game

Welcome to the Tic-Tac Game! This is a classic game known as Tic-Tac-Toe, where two players take turns marking the spaces in a 3Ã—3 grid with X and O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.

This game is designed to be simple and fun, perfect for passing time and exercising strategic thinking. Whether you're playing against a friend or simply practicing, we hope you enjoy the game!

Features

  • Interactive 3x3 grid
  • Real-time player turn display
  • Automatic win and draw detection
  • Easy game restart

How to Play

  1. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty cell.
  2. The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins the game.
  3. If all nine cells are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is declared a draw.
  4. Click the "Restart game" button to play again.
